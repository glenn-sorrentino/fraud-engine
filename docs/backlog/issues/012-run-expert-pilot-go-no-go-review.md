# Issue 012: Run Expert Pilot and Go/No-Go Review

Labels: `mvp`, `pilot`, `customer-discovery`, `priority:high`

Parent Epic: #1

## User Story

As a product team, we need expert review from real users to decide whether the MVP is worth continuing.

## Scope

Run a limited pilot with FCA counsel, procurement fraud investigators, or aerospace contracting experts.

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least 10 generated leads are reviewed by experts.
- Reviewers score each lead for novelty, legal relevance, explainability, actionability, and source quality.
- At least 2 leads are rated strong enough for deeper diligence to satisfy the MVP continuation threshold.
- Pilot produces a go/no-go/pivot decision memo.
- Decision memo includes pricing feedback and next build priorities.

- Issue 011.

## Notes

Pilot users should understand that outputs are investigative hypotheses, not fraud determinations or legal advice.
